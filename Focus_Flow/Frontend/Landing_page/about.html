Our Story

FocusFlow was born out of our own struggles with digital distractions. As developers and designers, we found ourselves constantly battling against the pull of social media, news sites, and other online distractions that were eating into our productivity.

We tried various website blockers and productivity tools, but none of them quite fit our needs. They were either too restrictive, too complicated, or lacked the features we wanted. So we decided to build our own solution.

What started as a simple tool for personal use quickly grew into something much bigger. Friends and colleagues started asking if they could use it too, and before we knew it, we had a growing community of users who were benefiting from our extension.

Today, FocusFlow helps thousands of people around the world take control of their digital lives, stay focused on what matters, and achieve their goals with less stress and more joy.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to help people be more present, focused, and productive in an increasingly distracting world. We believe that technology should work for you, not against you.

We're committed to creating tools that are not only effective but also a joy to use. We believe that the best productivity tools are the ones you actually want to use every day.
